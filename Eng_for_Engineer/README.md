### Polite and professional alternatives

Instead of saying “like I told you,” use softer, respectful phrases:

* **“Just to recap…”**
* **“Let me repeat that…”**
* **“As a reminder…”**
* **“To clarify again…”**
* **“Let me restate that…”**

These sound **helpful**, not blaming.
